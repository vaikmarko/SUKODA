Sinu rütm

Lepime kokku ajakohaselt — kas oled kodus või ei. Oluline on, et tuled koju ja kõik on tehtud. We agree on timing — whether you're home or not. What matters is that you come home and everything is done.

Meie sõna

Iga detail loeb. Kui tunned, et midagi saab veel paremini — tuleme tagasi ja viimistleme. Tasuta, küsimusteta. Every detail matters. If you feel something could be even better — we come back and perfect it. Free, no questions asked.

Meie vahel

Sinu kodu on sinu eraasi. Täielik diskreetsus — alati. Your home is your private matter. Complete discretion — always.

Esimene kord on eriline

Esimene hoolitsus on meie jaoks eriti oluline. Võtame aega, et tundma õppida iga nurka ja detaili — et sinu kodu saaks just selle hoole, mida ta väärib. Seetõttu kestab esimene külastus tavalisest kauem. The first visit is especially important to us. We take time to learn every corner and detail — so your home receives exactly the care it deserves. That's why the first visit takes longer than usual.

Sinu isiklik portaal

Sinu isiklikus portaalis saad hallata visiite, lisada eelistusi ja erisoove — et iga järgmine kord oleks veelgi parem. Koristaja näeb sinu soove ja arvestab nendega. In your personal portal you can manage visits, add preferences and special requests — so every next time is even better. Your cleaner sees your wishes and takes them into account.
