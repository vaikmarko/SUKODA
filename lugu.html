Algus

Maailma parimad hotellid teavad üht lihtsat tõde: tõeline luksus pole see, mida näed. See on see, mida tunned. The world's finest hotels understand a simple truth: true luxury is not what you see. It's what you feel.

Astud sisse ja kõik on paigas. Iga detail läbi mõeldud. Puhas, värske, laitmatu. Keegi on hoolinud enne sind.

You walk in and everything is in place. Every detail considered. Clean, fresh, immaculate. Someone has cared before you.

SUKODA sündis soovist tuua täpselt see tunne sinu koju.

SUKODA was born from the desire to bring exactly that feeling to your home.

Veendumus, et kodu väärib täpselt sama hoolikust nagu parim hotell maailmas. The conviction that home deserves exactly the same care as the finest hotel in the world.

( 02 )

Veendumus

Kodu on kõige isiklikum ruum. Home is the most intimate space.

Ainuke koht, mis on tõeliselt sinu. Kus hommikukohv maitseb kõige paremini. Kus jalad puutuvad põrandat ja sa tead: oled kodus.

The only space that's truly yours. Where morning coffee tastes best. Where your feet touch the floor and you know: you're home.

See koht väärib sama tähelepanu, mida koged maailma parimas hotellis. Sama hoolivust. Sama standardit.

This space deserves the same attention you experience at the world's finest hotel. The same devotion. The same standard.

Iga detail on valik. Need valikud räägivad sinust.

Every detail is a choice. Those choices speak of you.

Usaldus algab tuttavast näost. Üks inimene, kes tunneb sinu kodu ja hoolib sellest nii nagu sina.

Trust begins with a familiar face. One person who knows your home and cares for it the way you do.

Sinu kodu. Sinu standard. Meie hool. Your home. Your standard. Our care.

"Kodu väärib parimat.
Ja sa väärid kodu,
mis sinu eest hoolitseb." "Home deserves the best.
And you deserve a home
that takes care of you."
